# Natura Corporation - Teal Blue Color Scheme

## Brand Colors

### Primary Teal Blue
- **Primary**: `hsl(188, 85%, 35%)` - #0D9488 (Teal-600)
  - Main brand color used for buttons, links, and key UI elements
  - Professional, trustworthy, and modern

### Accent Bright Teal
- **Accent**: `hsl(186, 95%, 45%)` - #06B6D4 (Cyan-500)
  - Used for highlights, badges, and call-to-action elements
  - Vibrant and eye-catching while staying on brand

## Background Colors

### Cool Neutrals
- **Background**: `hsl(200, 20%, 98%)` - Very light blue-tinted white
  - Subtle cool tone that complements the teal palette
  - Creates a cohesive, professional look

- **Secondary Background**: `hsl(195, 20%, 92%)` - Light blue-gray
  - Used for section backgrounds and cards
  - Maintains the cool, professional aesthetic

## Typography Colors

### Text Colors
- **Foreground**: `hsl(195, 30%, 12%)` - Deep blue-black
  - Primary text color with a cool undertone
  - Excellent readability against light backgrounds

- **Muted Foreground**: `hsl(195, 15%, 40%)` - Medium blue-gray
  - Secondary text and descriptions
  - Softer contrast while staying on brand

## Subsidiary Company Colors

Each company uses a variation of teal/cyan to maintain brand cohesion:

1. **Natura Care** (Healthcare): `#14B8A6` - Teal-500
2. **Factopay** (Finance): `#06B6D4` - Cyan-500
3. **Cultural Escape Travels** (Travel): `#0891B2` - Cyan-600
4. **Natura Agroband** (Agriculture): `#0D9488` - Teal-600
5. **Natura Homes** (Real Estate): `#0EA5E9` - Sky-500

## Gradients

### Hero Gradient
```css
linear-gradient(135deg, hsl(188 85% 25%) 0%, hsl(190 80% 35%) 50%, hsl(186 75% 30%) 100%)
```
- Rich teal blue gradient for hero sections
- Creates depth and visual interest

### Accent Gradient
```css
linear-gradient(135deg, hsl(186 95% 45%) 0%, hsl(190 100% 50%) 100%)
```
- Bright teal to cyan gradient
- Used for buttons and highlighted elements

## Design Principles

1. **Cohesion**: All colors are in the blue-teal family (180-200° hue range)
2. **Professionalism**: Cool tones convey trust and stability
3. **Modernity**: Bright teals and cyans feel fresh and contemporary
4. **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios for text readability
5. **Consistency**: Every element uses the teal blue palette

## Color Psychology

- **Teal Blue**: Trust, professionalism, innovation, growth
- **Cyan**: Communication, clarity, technology, forward-thinking
- **Cool Neutrals**: Calm, sophisticated, reliable

This color scheme creates a unified, professional brand identity that's modern, trustworthy, and visually cohesive across all pages and components.
